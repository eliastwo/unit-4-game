Crystal Game

There are 4 crystals displayed on the screen. Each crystal has been assigned a random value. A target score is also displayed on the screen. Press the crystals to see the player score increment. The amount the score increments tells you the "hidden" value for that crystal. You can press each crystal as many times as you like. The object is to duplicate the target score by pressing the crystals and adding the value of each. Good luck.
